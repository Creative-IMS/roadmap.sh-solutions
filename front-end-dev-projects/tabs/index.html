Our Mission

Our mission is to keep the peace across the cosmos. This includes but is by no means limited to:

  • Acting as a neutral party for negotiations between different systems
  • Being at the forefront of agriculture and horiculture
  • Advancing technology for the betterment of all society
  • Keeping the bandits at bay
  • Saving kittens and puppies from high trees

Rules and Procedures

  1. Never take action without first talking with base command.
  2. Strive to be fair always.
  3. Safty first! Never do operations without taking the proper precautions!
  4. Never take bribes or monetary gains from any clients.
  5. Always upkeep the maintenance of your ship!
